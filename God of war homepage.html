INTRODUCTION

Starting with the release of God of war in 2005 on PlayStation2,Santa Monica Studio has created seven games across multiple platforms.Up to the most recent release in 2018,the franchise focused on Kratos' journey in the Greek world, following a path of vengeance as a result of being tricked into killing his family by the Olympian gods. Now,living as a man outside the shadow of the gods, Kratos must adapt to unfamiliar lands,unexpected threats,and a second chance at being a father.Together with his son Atreus,the pair will venture into the brutal Norse wilds and flight to fullfill a deeply personal quest
